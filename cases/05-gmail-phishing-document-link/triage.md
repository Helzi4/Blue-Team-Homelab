# Triage

## Initial Alert Source
The case began as a user-reported suspicious email.

## Initial Observations
The message used a document-sharing / invoice-review theme and asked the user to open a shared file.

## Why It Was Suspicious
- Unexpected invoice-related email
- Link-based lure
- Sender required validation
- The destination was not a trusted business document platform

## Initial Hypotheses
1. Benign internal business communication
2. Controlled phishing simulation
3. Phishing-style social engineering attempt with possible follow-on activity

## Initial Priority
Medium

## Scope at Triage Stage
- One mailbox
- One user
- One Windows endpoint: `10.10.10.100`
- One destination host: `10.10.10.101`
